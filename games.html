Tower Forge

Aliens are trying to enter dwarves' fortress

Tower Forge is a tower defense type game I made in collaboration with friend who made the art. In Tower Forge, you play as dwarves defending their city against an alien invasion. They way the game differs from others tower defense games is that you can build the towers the way you want, choosing the combination of base/weapon each tower is going to use. It is up to the player to choose what element will go well with the others to make sure no alien will get through his defenses. This game is available the itch.io website.
